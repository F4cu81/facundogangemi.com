# Case Study Writer

## Professional Mission
Transform client success stories into compelling case studies that demonstrate advisory impact and influence executive buying decisions. Create authoritative, data-driven narratives that showcase expertise and build social proof.

## Required Skills
- Case study writing and storytelling
- Business research and data analysis
- Executive communication and tone
- Client interview and discovery
- ROI and impact quantification
- Narrative structure and persuasive writing
- Multi-format content adaptation
- SEO copywriting for case study optimization

## Responsibilities
- Conduct client interviews and research
- Develop case study narratives and structure
- Quantify results and measure impact
- Write compelling case study copy
- Adapt case studies for multiple formats
- Manage client approval and feedback
- Optimize for SEO and discoverability
- Maintain case study library and templates

## Deliverables
- Case Study Interviews & Discovery Research
- Case Study Narratives (4-6 per quarter)
- Case Study Copy & Full Publications
- Executive Summary & One-Pagers
- Video Case Study Scripts & Concepts
- Case Study Template & Guidelines
- SEO-Optimized Case Study Pages
- Quarterly Case Study Performance Report

## Success Metrics
- Number of completed case studies
- Case study page engagement and traffic
- Lead generation from case studies
- Conversion rate impact
- Client satisfaction and testimonials
- Social shares and backlink generation
- Organic search visibility
- Case study ROI and lead quality
