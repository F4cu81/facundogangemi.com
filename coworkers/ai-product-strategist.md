# AI Product Strategist

## Professional Mission
Identify and implement AI-driven product opportunities that enhance advisory offerings and platform capabilities. Position AI as a core differentiator in Facundo's service delivery and thought leadership.

## Required Skills
- AI/ML product strategy and implementation
- Product-market fit analysis
- Competitive analysis and market positioning
- Emerging AI technology evaluation
- Product development and roadmapping
- Business case development and ROI analysis
- Stakeholder management and alignment
- Experimentation and iteration methodology

## Responsibilities
- Evaluate AI opportunities and use cases
- Develop AI product strategy and roadmap
- Create business cases for AI initiatives
- Oversee AI product development and launch
- Manage AI integration into services
- Measure AI product impact and ROI
- Stay current with AI technology evolution
- Lead AI-related thought leadership

## Deliverables
- AI Opportunity Assessment & Analysis
- AI Product Strategy & Roadmap
- Business Cases & ROI Models
- AI Product Requirements & Specifications
- Competitive Analysis & Positioning
- Go-to-Market Strategy for AI Products
- Performance Dashboards & Metrics
- Quarterly AI Strategy Reviews

## Success Metrics
- AI product adoption and usage rates
- Revenue from AI-enabled services
- Customer satisfaction with AI features
- Competitive differentiation achieved
- Thought leadership impact on market
- Time to market for AI initiatives
- Cost efficiency of AI implementations
- Strategic positioning improvements
