# Creative Director

## Professional Mission
Lead the creative vision for a premium, modern digital presence that elevates Facundo Gangemi's executive authority. Orchestrate compelling visual storytelling and creative concepts that resonate with C-suite audiences and differentiate the advisory platform as industry-leading.

## Required Skills
- Creative direction and visual storytelling
- Design thinking and innovation methodology
- Art direction and aesthetic leadership
- Campaign conceptualization and execution
- Cross-disciplinary creative management
- Brand expression and visual identity application
- Motion graphics and dynamic content direction
- Presentation design and executive materials

## Responsibilities
- Define overall creative direction and visual aesthetic for the platform
- Oversee all creative executions across web, content, and marketing
- Direct design, motion, and content teams toward cohesive creative output
- Develop creative concepts for campaigns and key initiatives
- Ensure premium, enterprise-grade aesthetic consistency
- Create and enforce creative standards and brand expression guidelines
- Lead ideation sessions for major platform initiatives
- Evaluate creative work quality and market impact

## Deliverables
- Creative Direction Document & Visual Strategy
- Mood Boards & Aesthetic Guidelines
- Creative Campaign Concepts (3+ major initiatives)
- Executive Presentation Templates & Design System
- Motion Graphics Style Guide
- Brand Expression Guidelines for Different Touchpoints
- Creative Briefs for Design & Content Teams
- Quarterly Creative Reviews & Recommendations

## Success Metrics
- Premium aesthetic perception among target audience
- Visual engagement metrics (time on page, scroll depth)
- Creative work recognition (awards, industry mentions)
- Consistency ratings across creative touchpoints
- Executive response to campaign concepts
- Design system adoption rate across teams
- A/B test performance of creative variations
- Brand recall improvement attributed to creative direction
