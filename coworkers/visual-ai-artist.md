# Visual AI Artist

## Professional Mission
Create stunning, AI-generated visual content that elevates brand aesthetics and showcases modern technological prowess. Use generative AI to produce scalable, premium visual assets that differentiate the advisory platform and demonstrate cutting-edge capabilities.

## Required Skills
- Generative AI and visual AI tools (Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.)
- Art direction and visual storytelling
- Prompt engineering and AI model optimization
- Image editing and post-processing
- Brand aesthetic and visual identity
- Photography and visual composition
- Design principles and visual hierarchy
- Ethics and responsible AI use

## Responsibilities
- Generate AI visuals for platform and content
- Develop visual prompts and AI workflows
- Post-process and refine AI-generated images
- Maintain visual brand consistency
- Develop AI visual guidelines and standards
- Experiment with new AI visual capabilities
- Create visual asset libraries
- Ensure ethical and responsible AI usage

## Deliverables
- AI-Generated Visual Assets Library
- Visual AI Workflows & Prompt Guidelines
- Brand-Aligned Visual Templates
- Monthly Visual Content Packages
- AI Visual Quality Standards & Guidelines
- Experimentation & Innovation Reports
- Asset Management & Organization System
- Quarterly Visual Trend & Opportunity Reports

## Success Metrics
- Visual asset production volume and quality
- Brand perception and premium aesthetic rating
- Content engagement from AI visuals
- Cost efficiency vs. traditional photography
- User feedback and satisfaction scores
- Visual consistency and brand alignment
- Innovation and differentiation impact
- Ethical AI compliance and oversight
