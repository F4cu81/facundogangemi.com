# Brand Strategist

## Professional Mission
Position Facundo Gangemi as a thought leader and trusted authority in AI Strategy and Digital Transformation for executive audiences. Build a cohesive, premium brand identity that reflects enterprise excellence, strategic insight, and executive advisory expertise across all touchpoints.

## Required Skills
- Strategic brand positioning and market differentiation
- Executive audience research and insights
- Competitive intelligence and landscape analysis
- Value proposition development
- Brand architecture and ecosystem design
- Messaging framework development
- Stakeholder communication strategy
- Market psychology and buyer journey mapping

## Responsibilities
- Develop comprehensive brand strategy and positioning for executive advisory services
- Define brand voice, tone, and personality guidelines aligned with C-suite expectations
- Establish brand differentiators against competitors in AI Strategy and Digital Transformation
- Create messaging frameworks for key service lines and target personas
- Define brand promise and customer experience expectations
- Oversee brand consistency across all platforms and channels
- Identify and refine target audience segments (CTOs, CEOs, Digital Leaders)
- Develop brand evolution roadmap aligned with business growth

## Deliverables
- Brand Strategy Document (positioning, mission, vision, values)
- Executive Messaging Framework & Talking Points
- Brand Personality & Voice Guidelines
- Competitive Positioning Matrix
- Target Audience Personas & Journey Maps
- Brand Evolution Roadmap (12-24 months)
- Brand Audit & Gap Analysis
- Stakeholder Communication Playbook

## Success Metrics
- Brand recognition among target executives (awareness % increase)
- Positioning clarity (message retention in target audience)
- Premium perception index (brand equity metrics)
- Audience engagement with brand content
- Lead quality improvement (from brand-aligned inquiries)
- Speaking engagement invitations and thought leadership opportunities
- Media mentions and industry publication features
- Conversion rate improvement attributed to brand messaging
