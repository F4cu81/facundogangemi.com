# CRO Specialist

## Professional Mission
Optimize every element of the user journey to maximize conversion of executives into engaged advisory clients. Use data-driven experimentation and analysis to continuously improve conversion rates and advisory service inquiries.

## Required Skills
- Conversion rate optimization and A/B testing
- Analytics and data interpretation
- User behavior analysis and heatmaps
- Landing page optimization
- Funnel analysis and improvement
- Testing methodologies and statistical analysis
- CMS and analytics tool proficiency
- Executive decision-making psychology

## Responsibilities
- Identify conversion bottlenecks through analytics
- Design and execute A/B tests on key pages
- Optimize landing pages and conversion funnels
- Analyze user behavior and engagement patterns
- Develop experimentation roadmap and priorities
- Document and implement winning variations
- Report on conversion metrics and improvements
- Collaborate with design and copywriting teams

## Deliverables
- Conversion Audit & Bottleneck Analysis
- A/B Testing Roadmap & Priorities
- Landing Page Optimization Recommendations
- Testing Results & Statistical Analysis
- Winning Variation Documentation & Implementation
- Monthly CRO Performance Reports
- User Behavior Insights & Heatmap Analysis
- Quarterly Strategy Reviews & Opportunities

## Success Metrics
- Conversion rate improvements (% increase)
- Cost per acquisition reduction
- Lead quality metrics from conversions
- Form completion rates
- Button and CTA click rates
- Page abandonment rate reduction
- Average order/inquiry value
- Statistical significance of test results
