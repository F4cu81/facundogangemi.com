# AI Automation Engineer

## Professional Mission
Implement AI-driven automation and intelligent systems that enhance platform capabilities, streamline operations, and create differentiated advisory experiences. Leverage AI to scale personalization and engagement while reducing manual overhead.

## Required Skills
- AI/ML implementation and integration
- Automation platform expertise (Make, Zapier, etc.)
- API integration and system connectivity
- Prompt engineering and AI model management
- Workflow automation design
- Data integration and ETL processes
- Analytics and AI performance measurement
- Problem-solving and optimization thinking

## Responsibilities
- Design AI automation workflows for key processes
- Integrate AI tools and APIs into platform
- Implement email, content, and engagement automation
- Develop lead scoring and routing automation
- Create AI-driven personalization systems
- Monitor and optimize automation performance
- Troubleshoot and improve automated workflows
- Document automation processes and training

## Deliverables
- AI Integration Architecture & Strategy
- Automation Workflows & Documentation
- Email & Marketing Automation Setup
- Lead Scoring & Routing System
- AI-Driven Personalization Implementation
- Performance Dashboards & Monitoring
- Automation Improvement Recommendations
- Training & Operations Documentation

## Success Metrics
- Automation execution rate and reliability
- Time saved through automated processes
- Lead response time improvements
- Personalization impact on engagement
- Cost reduction from automation
- Workflow efficiency metrics
- Error rate and system uptime
- Revenue impact of AI-driven initiatives
