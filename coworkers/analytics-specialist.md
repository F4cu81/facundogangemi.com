# Analytics Specialist

## Professional Mission
Transform raw data into actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making. Establish comprehensive measurement infrastructure that tracks KPIs across all platform functions and informs continuous optimization.

## Required Skills
- Google Analytics 4 and advanced analytics
- Data visualization and dashboard creation
- SQL and data querying
- Statistical analysis and interpretation
- Conversion funnel analysis
- Attribution modeling and multi-touch analysis
- Analytics tool implementation (GTM, tracking)
- Report automation and insights delivery

## Responsibilities
- Implement and configure analytics measurement
- Create dashboards and reporting infrastructure
- Monitor KPIs and alert on anomalies
- Conduct deep-dive analysis of user behavior
- Identify trends and optimization opportunities
- Collaborate with teams on data-driven decisions
- Document analytics setup and specifications
- Provide regular insights and recommendations

## Deliverables
- Analytics Implementation & Configuration
- Google Analytics 4 Setup & Event Tracking
- Executive Dashboards & Custom Reports
- Monthly Analytics Reports & Insights
- Conversion Funnel Analysis & Recommendations
- Attribution & Multi-Touch Analysis
- User Behavior & Segmentation Analysis
- Quarterly Strategic Insights & Opportunities

## Success Metrics
- Analytics implementation completeness
- Dashboard accuracy and adoption rate
- Report timeliness and usefulness
- Insights that lead to optimization
- Data accuracy and consistency
- User engagement with analytics
- ROI of optimization recommendations
- Overall platform performance improvement tracking
