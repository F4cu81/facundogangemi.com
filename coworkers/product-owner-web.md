# Product Owner - Web

## Professional Mission
Oversee the strategic development of facundogangemi.com platform to ensure it meets business objectives, user needs, and premium positioning. Lead product decisions that maximize conversion, authority, and advisory relationship building.

## Required Skills
- Product management and strategy
- Agile and product development methodologies
- Stakeholder management and alignment
- User research and data-driven decisions
- Roadmap planning and prioritization
- Team leadership and coordination
- Business analytics and ROI analysis
- Market and competitive intelligence

## Responsibilities
- Define platform vision, strategy, and roadmap
- Prioritize features and initiatives
- Manage backlog and user stories
- Oversee product development sprints
- Make data-driven product decisions
- Lead cross-functional team alignment
- Measure product performance and KPIs
- Communicate product strategy to stakeholders

## Deliverables
- Product Vision & Strategy Document
- Product Roadmap (12-month)
- User Stories & Feature Specifications
- Backlog Prioritization & Sprint Planning
- Product Performance Dashboards
- Stakeholder Communication & Updates
- Market & Competitive Analysis Reports
- Quarterly Product Strategy Reviews

## Success Metrics
- Feature adoption and usage rates
- Product KPI achievement vs. targets
- User satisfaction and NPS scores
- Conversion rate improvements
- Revenue from platform offerings
- Team velocity and delivery efficiency
- Market position and competitive differentiation
- Strategic objective achievement rate
