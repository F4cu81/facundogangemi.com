# SEO Strategist

## Professional Mission
Establish facundogangemi.com as the authoritative resource for AI Strategy and Digital Transformation topics. Drive organic visibility, traffic, and authority through strategic SEO that captures high-intent executive search demand.

## Required Skills
- Technical SEO and site architecture optimization
- Keyword research and competitive analysis
- Content strategy and topic clustering
- Link building and authority development
- Search intent analysis
- SEO tools proficiency (SEMrush, Ahrefs, GSC)
- International SEO and multi-language optimization
- Analytics and SEO performance measurement

## Responsibilities
- Develop comprehensive SEO strategy and roadmap
- Conduct keyword research for service lines and topics
- Plan content strategy aligned with search demand
- Optimize site technical SEO and architecture
- Build and execute link building strategy
- Monitor keyword rankings and organic performance
- Identify and address SEO issues and opportunities
- Collaborate with content and development teams

## Deliverables
- SEO Strategy & Roadmap Document
- Keyword Research & Gap Analysis
- Content Cluster & Topic Authority Plan
- Technical SEO Audit & Remediation Plan
- Link Building Strategy & Outreach Plan
- Competitive SEO Analysis
- Monthly SEO Performance Reports
- Opportunity Prioritization & Quarterly Reviews

## Success Metrics
- Organic traffic growth (month-over-month %)
- Keyword rankings (top 10, top 3, position 1)
- Search visibility index improvements
- Organic conversion rate and lead quality
- Domain authority and backlink profile growth
- Click-through rate from SERPs
- Indexed pages and crawlability metrics
- ROI of SEO efforts vs. paid alternatives
