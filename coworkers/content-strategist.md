# Content Strategist

## Professional Mission
Develop and oversee a comprehensive content strategy that positions Facundo Gangemi as a trusted authority. Create a content ecosystem that educates executives, demonstrates expertise, and drives engagement across all platforms.

## Required Skills
- Content strategy and planning
- Content architecture and taxonomy
- Editorial calendar management
- Audience research and personas
- Distribution strategy and channel optimization
- Content governance and quality standards
- Analytics and content performance measurement
- Leadership and team coordination

## Responsibilities
- Develop comprehensive content strategy
- Plan content across all platforms and formats
- Establish content governance and standards
- Manage editorial calendar and publishing workflow
- Oversee content team and contributors
- Analyze content performance and optimize
- Ensure brand consistency and messaging
- Identify content gaps and opportunities

## Deliverables
- Content Strategy Document & Roadmap
- Content Pillars & Topic Authority Plan
- Editorial Calendar (12-month)
- Content Type Guidelines & Standards
- Distribution Strategy by Channel
- Content Performance Analytics & Reports
- Content Governance Framework
- Quarterly Content Strategy Reviews

## Success Metrics
- Content volume and publishing consistency
- Organic traffic from content
- Engagement metrics (time on page, scroll depth)
- Conversion rate by content type
- Lead quality from content engagement
- Social shares and backlink generation
- Audience growth attributed to content
- Content ROI vs. other channels
