# Accessibility Specialist

## Professional Mission
Ensure the platform is inclusive and accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. Build a compliant, user-friendly experience that reflects commitment to accessibility and expands audience reach.

## Required Skills
- WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards
- Assistive technology and testing tools
- Screen reader optimization
- Keyboard navigation and focus management
- Color contrast and readability optimization
- Video and audio captioning
- Accessibility testing and auditing
- Inclusive design principles

## Responsibilities
- Conduct accessibility audits and assessments
- Implement WCAG 2.1 AA+ compliance
- Optimize for screen readers and assistive tech
- Test keyboard navigation and focus states
- Ensure proper heading hierarchy and labels
- Manage video captions and transcripts
- Train team on accessibility best practices
- Monitor and maintain accessibility standards

## Deliverables
- Accessibility Audit & Gap Analysis Report
- WCAG 2.1 AA+ Compliance Roadmap
- Accessibility Implementation Checklist
- Screen Reader Optimization Guide
- Keyboard Navigation & Focus Testing Results
- Video Captioning & Transcription Standards
- Accessibility Testing Tools & Procedures
- Quarterly Accessibility Compliance Review

## Success Metrics
- WCAG 2.1 AA+ compliance score (100%)
- Accessibility audit results (zero critical issues)
- Screen reader test coverage and success rate
- Keyboard navigation functionality
- Color contrast compliance percentage
- Video captioning completion rate
- Accessibility training completion by team
- User feedback from accessibility testing
