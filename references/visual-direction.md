# Visual Direction — facundogangemi.com

## 1. Strategic Purpose

This visual direction defines the aesthetic, positioning and design criteria for `facundogangemi.com`.

The website must position Facundo Gangemi as an executive-level AI Strategy & Enterprise Transformation leader, focused on helping organizations evolve through artificial intelligence, operating model transformation, digital execution and operational excellence.

The site should communicate credibility, strategic clarity, executive authority and enterprise-grade sophistication.

---

## 2. Brand Positioning

The website should represent Facundo Gangemi as:

- AI Strategy & Transformation Leader
- Enterprise Transformation Advisor
- Operating Model Strategist
- Digital Transformation Executive
- Product & Technology Transformation Specialist
- Operational Excellence Consultant
- Trusted advisor for banking, financial services and enterprise organizations

The positioning should feel closer to an executive advisor or transformation director than to a freelancer, coach or traditional consultant.

---

## 3. Target Audience

The visual language must be designed for:

- CEOs
- CIOs
- CTOs
- Transformation Directors
- Product Leaders
- Technology Leaders
- Banking Executives
- Financial Services Leaders
- Enterprise Decision Makers
- AI Governance Leaders
- Organizations evaluating AI and digital transformation initiatives

Every page should feel credible enough for senior enterprise stakeholders.

---

## 4. Desired Visual Feel

The website must feel:

- Executive
- Premium
- Strategic
- AI-native
- Modern
- Minimal
- Sophisticated
- Enterprise-grade
- Futuristic but credible
- Cinematic but not exaggerated
- Dark, elegant and high-end
- Editorial and thought-leadership oriented

The visual system should suggest strategic intelligence, transformation leadership and business impact.

---

## 5. Core Aesthetic Direction

The preferred aesthetic combines:

- Premium dark UI
- Editorial typography
- Large strategic headlines
- Strong spacing
- Clean asymmetric layouts
- AI-inspired gradients
- Subtle glow effects
- Minimal glassmorphism
- Enterprise dashboard references
- Executive photography
- High-end consulting credibility

The design should blend the feeling of:

- OpenAI
- Linear
- Vercel
- Apple
- Palantir
- Stripe
- Arc Browser
- McKinsey Digital
- BCG X
- Accenture Song

The site should feel modern and AI-native, but still appropriate for enterprise executives.

---

## 6. Primary Color Direction

Preferred palette:

- Matte Black: `#070B14`
- Midnight Navy: `#081120`
- Deep Navy Blue: `#00274C`
- Electric Cyan: `#00D9FF`
- Neon Blue: `#4CC9FF`
- Graphite Gray: `#1C1F26`
- Soft Light Gray: `#F2F2F2`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

The main accent should be electric cyan or electric blue.

Orange may be used only as a secondary or legacy accent when needed, but the preferred direction for the new personal brand is electric cyan / blue.

Avoid using too many accent colors at once.

---

## 7. Typography Direction

Typography should feel premium, editorial and executive.

Guidelines:

- Use large, confident headlines.
- Prioritize strong visual hierarchy.
- Combine modern sans-serif typography with editorial-style headlines when appropriate.
- Avoid playful, decorative or overly casual fonts.
- Maintain high readability across all pages.
- Use generous line-height and spacing.
- Keep paragraphs concise and elegant.

The typography should reinforce strategic authority and thought leadership.

---

## 8. Layout Principles

The layout should follow these principles:

- Large hero sections with strong positioning.
- Generous whitespace and breathing room.
- Premium section transitions.
- Clear separation between strategic blocks.
- Minimal but powerful visual elements.
- Avoid cluttered card grids.
- Use asymmetric compositions when they add sophistication.
- Maintain responsive behavior across desktop, tablet and mobile.
- Prioritize visual clarity over decoration.

The site should feel intentional, calm and high-status.

---

## 9. Photography Direction

Use uploaded photos of Facundo naturally within the website.

Photography should feel:

- Executive
- Cinematic
- Premium
- Professional
- Strategic
- Confident but approachable
- Enterprise-oriented

Recommended placements:

- Homepage hero section
- About page leadership section
- Article author profile
- Contact page executive CTA
- Thought leadership areas

Image treatment:

- Dark premium lighting
- Elegant cropping
- Subtle depth of field
- AI-inspired glow accents when appropriate
- Editorial composition
- No exaggerated retouching
- No artificial-looking portraits

Photos must reinforce executive authority and enterprise credibility.

---

## 10. Page-Level Direction

### Home Page

The homepage should create immediate executive authority.

It should include:

- Premium navigation
- Cinematic hero section
- Strong AI Strategy positioning
- Enterprise transformation message
- Strategic advisory CTA
- Services / expertise areas
- Enterprise impact metrics
- Industries served
- Thought leadership preview
- Premium footer

The home page should feel like the entry point to an executive advisory brand.

### Article Page

The article page should feel editorial, premium and highly readable.

It should include:

- Large article hero
- Estimated reading time
- Author profile
- Sticky table of contents when appropriate
- Pull quotes
- Clean content hierarchy
- Related articles
- Newsletter or insight subscription CTA

The article page should support thought leadership around AI Strategy, Digital Transformation, Operational Excellence, Enterprise Agility and Product Transformation.

### About Page

The about page should position Facundo as an executive transformation leader.

It should include:

- Executive portrait
- Strategic leadership narrative
- Career highlights
- Enterprise transformation experience
- Banking and financial services expertise
- MIT-related education reference
- Operating model expertise
- Leadership philosophy
- Timeline-style professional evolution

The page should communicate trust, authority and strategic depth.

### Contact Page

The contact page should feel exclusive, premium and executive.

It should include:

- Strategic consultation CTA
- Minimal contact form
- Enterprise inquiry messaging
- Clear business context fields
- Executive scheduling area if needed
- Premium footer

The contact experience should feel high-value, not transactional or generic.

## 12. UI Details

Preferred UI details:

- Subtle cyan glow accents
- Thin borders with low opacity
- Premium dark cards
- Elegant glassmorphism only when useful
- Soft gradients
- Minimal icons
- Executive metric blocks
- Dashboard-inspired data visuals
- Clean CTA buttons
- Hover states that feel refined
- Smooth but subtle animations

Avoid excessive animation or visual noise.

---

## 13. Anti-Patterns to Avoid

The website must NOT look like:

- A freelancer portfolio
- A motivational coach website
- A generic Agile Coach page
- A startup landing page template
- A marketing agency website
- A loud personal brand website
- A colorful bootcamp page
- A generic SaaS template
- A corporate stock-photo website
- A design overloaded with cards and gradients

Avoid:

- Excessive orange
- Overly playful illustrations
- Cartoon visuals
- Generic business icons
- Cheap stock photos
- Too many CTAs
- Crowded sections
- Low-contrast text
- Template-looking layouts
- Buzzword-heavy sections without strategic clarity

## 15. Development Guidance

When implementing the design in Astro/Tailwind:

- Prioritize clean component structure.
- Keep styles reusable and consistent.
- Use semantic HTML.
- Optimize for SEO.
- Maintain accessibility standards.
- Ensure excellent performance.
- Avoid unnecessary libraries.
- Keep animations subtle and purposeful.
- Build responsive layouts from the beginning.

The implementation should be production-ready, maintainable and aligned with a premium executive brand.
