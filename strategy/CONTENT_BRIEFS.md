## 0. Global rules (apply to every page)

**Brand voice:** strategic · executive · intelligent · pragmatic · anti-hype · human.

**Reading layer model:** *skim layer* (eyebrow, H2, bullets, KPIs) + *deep layer* (paragraphs, quotes, frameworks). Both must work independently.

**Language:** EN primary at launch to support international executive positioning. ES becomes the secondary language and will be added under `/es/` in a later phase. All briefs below are language-agnostic — copy direction works in both. Existing Spanish SEO equity must be preserved through redirects, equivalent Spanish pages, or static redirect pages where appropriate.

**Identity evolution principle:** every page must reinforce the move from *Agile/Digital Coach* → *AI Strategy Advisor · Enterprise Transformation Consultant · Executive AI Transformation Leader*.

**SEO continuity:** preserve existing Spanish URL equity where possible. The new site launches in English at root-level routes, while future Spanish content should live under `/es/`. Insights and Case Studies inherit blog/projects authority, but Spanish legacy URLs must be mapped carefully to future `/es/insights/` or `/es/case-studies/` equivalents when available.

**Conversion model:** every page exits via either (1) Book Advisory Call (high intent), (2) AI Maturity Assessment (mid intent), or (3) Newsletter / Insights (low intent).

**Emotional throughline:** *"This person is at my level — and ahead of where my organization needs to be."*
