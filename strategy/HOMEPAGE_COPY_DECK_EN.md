# 0. Copy Principles

The homepage copy must position Facundo Gangemi as an executive advisor for AI Strategy and Enterprise Transformation.

The copy must avoid:

- generic consulting language
- AI hype
- excessive Agile/Scrum terminology
- motivational coaching tone
- freelancer-style positioning
- technical AI jargon without business relevance

The copy must communicate:

- executive confidence
- strategic clarity
- enterprise experience
- transformation maturity
- AI as business capability, not as a technology trend
- execution as the differentiator

Core narrative:

> Organizations do not transform with AI alone. They transform when strategy, operating models, leadership and execution evolve together.
