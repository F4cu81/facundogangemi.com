# VOICE AND TONE — facundogangemi.com

# Brand Voice

The communication style must be:
- strategic
- executive
- intelligent
- modern
- human
- pragmatic
- transformational

The tone should communicate:
- confidence
- clarity
- seniority
- strategic depth

---

# Communication Style

Preferred communication:
- concise
- intelligent
- outcome-oriented
- business-focused

Avoid:
- motivational clichés
- AI hype
- excessive buzzwords
- startup slang
- overly technical language

---

# Tone Characteristics

## Strategic
Communicates business transformation and long-term thinking.

## Executive
Feels premium and enterprise-oriented.

## Human
Remains approachable and clear.

## Pragmatic
Focuses on execution and business reality.

## Intelligent
Reflects expertise without sounding academic.

---

# Messaging Priorities

The messaging should focus on:
- business outcomes
- transformation
- operating models
- AI adoption
- enterprise evolution
- execution capability

Avoid messaging focused only on:
- Scrum
- Agile ceremonies
- frameworks
- methodologies

---

# Writing Style

Preferred:
- short paragraphs
- high readability
- strategic headlines
- clear calls to action

Avoid:
- long academic blocks
- generic consulting language
- exaggerated claims
