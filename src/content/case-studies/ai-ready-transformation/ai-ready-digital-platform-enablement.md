## Executive Summary

A digital platform initiative needed to move beyond isolated digital features and create a stronger foundation for scalable services, automation and future AI adoption.

The work focused on connecting platform design, process automation, data-informed decisions and operating routines. Instead of positioning AI as a separate technology layer, the intervention treated AI readiness as an operating model challenge.

## Context

The transformation context involved digital services, learning platforms and healthcare technology environments where users expected more personalized, efficient and data-driven experiences.

The organization needed to manage content, workflows, users, operational data and service delivery in a more scalable way. This required more than technology implementation. It required clearer processes, stronger data flows and better alignment between platform capabilities and business objectives.

## Business Challenge

The main challenge was not the lack of ambition around AI. The challenge was readiness.

The operating environment needed stronger foundations before advanced automation or AI-enabled capabilities could generate consistent business value. Key issues included fragmented workflows, limited visibility across operational data and a need to connect platform decisions with measurable outcomes.

Without that foundation, AI adoption risked becoming a set of isolated experiments rather than a strategic capability.

## Strategic Intervention

The intervention focused on preparing the digital platform environment for scale.

Facundo helped shape the logic behind digital services, course and content workflows, automation opportunities, data analysis needs and user experience improvements. The work connected business objectives with practical platform capabilities.

The advisory value was in translating AI and digital ambition into concrete operating elements: workflows, data inputs, decision points, governance routines and scalable execution practices.

## Execution Model

The execution model combined digital transformation and AI-readiness practices:

- Mapping digital service workflows.
- Identifying automation opportunities.
- Structuring platform capabilities around user and business needs.
- Connecting data analysis with decision-making.
- Supporting scalable content and service delivery.
- Aligning technology execution with business value.
- Preparing the operating model for future AI-enabled personalization and optimization.

## Business Impact

The initiatives created a clearer foundation for digital scale and AI adoption.

The organization improved its ability to structure digital services, analyze operational needs and connect platform execution with business outcomes. It also strengthened the conditions required for future AI-enabled automation, personalization and decision support.

The impact remains qualitative until specific performance metrics are validated for publication.

## Strategic Proof

This case demonstrates that AI strategy does not start with tools. It starts with the operating conditions that allow AI to create value.

Facundo's contribution shows the ability to connect platform thinking, digital transformation, automation readiness and operating model design. This is especially relevant for organizations that want to adopt AI but still need to clarify processes, data flows, ownership and execution routines.
