## Executive Summary

A technology delivery environment needed stronger operational discipline across projects, teams and management practices.

The intervention focused on improving execution consistency, delivery governance, Jira usage, playbooks, training and operational visibility. The work helped create a clearer foundation for leadership decision-making and day-to-day delivery management.

## Context

Technology delivery organizations often grow faster than their operating routines.

As teams, projects and stakeholders increase, inconsistent practices can create execution noise. Leaders may struggle to understand delivery health, risk exposure, work allocation and progress across initiatives.

The organization needed to move from dispersed practices toward a more disciplined operational model.

## Business Challenge

The main challenge was inconsistent operational visibility.

Project and delivery practices varied by team. Jira usage was not always standardized. Leadership lacked a clear view of delivery health, work distribution and operational risks. Teams also needed practical guidance to adopt common ways of working.

The business needed more reliable execution signals without creating unnecessary bureaucracy.

## Strategic Intervention

Facundo supported the design of operational playbooks, delivery practices and team enablement routines.

The work helped standardize how teams used Jira, how project information was structured, how progress was reviewed and how leadership could interpret delivery signals. Training and coaching supported adoption across teams and roles.

The intervention connected operational discipline with business decision-making.

## Execution Model

The execution model included:

- Operational playbook design.
- Jira adoption and usage standards.
- Team training and enablement.
- Delivery governance routines.
- PMO and delivery management practices.
- Work visibility and reporting logic.
- Dashboard-oriented decision support.
- Continuous improvement routines.

## Business Impact

The organization improved execution consistency and strengthened its ability to manage delivery across teams.

Leaders gained clearer visibility into delivery health and operational risks. Teams also gained practical guidance to work with more consistent routines, shared language and better information quality.

The impact remains qualitative until operational metrics are validated for publication.

## Strategic Proof

This case demonstrates Facundo's ability to transform operational complexity into clear routines, governance practices and management visibility.

It reinforces his positioning in Operational Excellence and delivery governance, especially for organizations that need to improve execution discipline before scaling transformation or AI initiatives.
